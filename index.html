About this dataset

Every year since 1960, True Parents have proclaimed an annual motto — a single sentence summarising the providential theme for that year. These mottos guide the spiritual and practical work of blessed families worldwide.

This dataset is the first open, machine-readable form of the complete corpus. Fetch motto.json and read data.mottos[] — each entry has year, motto_en, optionally motto_ko, details, and image.
